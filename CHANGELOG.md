## Release Notes

### Version 1.0.0 Highlights

This is the initial production release of ADAS Landing, featuring a complete landing page and SaaS platform for drone automation services. The platform includes:

- **Full-featured landing page** with modern design
- **E-commerce functionality** with Stripe integration
- **Customer portal** for managing subscriptions
- **Admin dashboard** for content management
- **Blog system** for content marketing
- **Career portal** for recruiting
- **Comprehensive documentation** for developers
